What Motivated Us?

Living in the city, we saw how much space was going to waste while people struggled with the high cost of fresh vegetables. This motivated us to explore urban farming—an affordable, sustainable solution to food security.

Our Vision

We aim to transform every balcony, rooftop, and backyard into a green space where food can be grown. Our dream is to build a self-sustaining community that values health, environment, and togetherness.
